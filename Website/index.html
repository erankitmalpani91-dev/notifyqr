How It Works

Scan QR. Message. Done.

No App. No Login

Finder Scans Your QR

Someone finds your car blocking, your lost keys, your pet — they point their phone camera at the QR sticker. No app. No account. Works on any phone.

⏱ Done in 3 seconds

2 💬

You Get a WhatsApp Message

A simple form opens on the finder's phone. They type their message — you receive it instantly on WhatsApp with their note and optional location.

Chat back on WhatsApp however you like. The finder never sees your phone number at any point. You stay fully in control.
