Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1st March 2026

ReachOutOwner (“Company”, “we”, “us”, “our”) operates a QR-based safety and contact protection platform that enables secure communication between asset owners and third parties without exposing personal phone numbers.

We are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act), the Information Technology Act, 2000, and applicable Indian regulations.

1. Personal Data We Collect

We may collect the following categories of data:

  • Full name
  • Mobile phone number
  • Email address
  • Shipping address
  • Payment transaction metadata (processed securely via Paytm)
  • QR asset registration details
  • QR scan logs (timestamp, issue category selected)
  • Owner response logs
  • Device and IP metadata for security purposes

We do not publicly display your personal phone number when a QR is scanned.

2. Purpose of Processing

Your data is processed strictly for:

  • Activating QR protection services
  • Sending WhatsApp alerts and missed call notifications
  • Enabling secure one-time reply communication
  • Processing payments and subscription renewals
  • Shipping physical QR stickers
  • Customer support and dispute resolution
  • Fraud prevention and abuse detection
  • Compliance with legal obligations

3. Legal Basis

Processing is based on:

  • Your explicit consent
  • Performance of service contract
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Legitimate interest in platform security

4. Data Sharing

We may share limited personal data with:

  • Payment gateway providers (Paytm)
  • Messaging service providers (WhatsApp Business API partners)
  • Telecom operators (for missed call infrastructure)
  • Shipping and logistics partners
  • Cloud hosting providers

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data.

5. Data Retention

Personal data is retained during the active subscription period and as required by applicable law. Expired QR logs may be archived securely for audit and compliance purposes.

6. User Rights Under DPDP Act

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction
  • Request deletion
  • Withdraw consent
  • Nominate another individual to exercise rights

To exercise your rights, contact: support@reachoutowner.com

7. Security Measures

We implement:

  • HTTPS encryption
  • Secure token-based QR system
  • Access control mechanisms
  • Database security controls
  • Session authentication
  • Periodic security review
